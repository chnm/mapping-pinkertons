Mapping the Pinkertons

A digital history project on the Pinkerton Detective Agency

Image: Wikimedia Commons

The Pinkerton National Detective Agency shaped American law enforcement, labor relations, and security practices from 1850 through the early 20th century. This digital project maps their operations, agents, and impact across the United States.

Interactive Mapping

Explore Pinkerton operations across America with our interactive map showing detective locations, cases, and historical movements.

Detective Profiles

Discover the stories of individual Pinkerton agents, their backgrounds, specialties, and the cases that defined their careers.

Data Analysis

Analyze patterns in Pinkerton operations through visualizations and statistical insights drawn from historical records.

Developed by the Roy Rosenzweig Center for History and New Media at George Mason University .
